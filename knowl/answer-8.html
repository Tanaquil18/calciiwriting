Answer 1.2.4.1.

  1. Partial fraction decomposition can only be performed when the degree of the numerator is strictly less than the degree of the denominator.
  2. \(\displaystyle \int x^2 - 2 x + 3 +\frac{3 x^3 + 5 x^2 + 12 x - 4}{x^4-16} \ dx\)
  3. \begin{equation*} \frac{x^3}{3} - x^2 + 3x + \int \frac{A_1}{x+2}+\frac{A_2}{x-2}+\frac{A_3x+A_4}{x^2+4}\ dx \end{equation*}
    which integrates to
    \begin{equation*} \frac{x^3}{3} - x^2 + 3x + A_1\ln|x+2|+A_2\ln|x-2|+\frac{A_3}{2}\ln|x^2+4|+ \frac{A_4}{2}\arctan\left(\frac{x}{2}\right)+C \end{equation*}
  4. \begin{equation*} \frac{x^3}{3} - x^2 + 3x + \ln|x+2|+2\ln|x-2|+ \frac{3}{2}\arctan\left(\frac{x}{2}\right)+C \end{equation*}
