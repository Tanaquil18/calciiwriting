    2. \begin{equation*} \int \frac{-x^2-x-3}{x^3+x}\, dx = A_1\ln|x|+\frac{A_2}{2}\ln|x^2+1|+A_3\arctan(x)+C \end{equation*}
    3. \(\displaystyle -3\ln|x|+\ln|x^2+1|-\arctan(x)+C\)
    1. \(x^5+x^4=x^4(x+1)\text{,}\) so \(\frac{x^4+1}{x^5+x^4}=\frac{A_1}{x}+\frac{A_2}{x^2}+\frac{A_3}{x^3} + \frac{A_4}{x^4}+\frac{A_5}{x+1}\)
    2. \begin{equation*} \int \frac{x^4+1}{x^5+x^4}\, dx = A_1\ln|x|+\frac{-A_2}{x}+\frac{-A_3}{2x^2} + \frac{-A_4}{3x^3}+A_5\ln|x+1| + C \end{equation*}
    3. We highly recommend substituting \(x=0\) and \(x=-1\) and then comparing like coefficients. \(-\ln|x|+\frac{-1}{x}+\frac{1}{2x^2} + \frac{-1}{3x^3}+2\ln|x+1| + C\)
